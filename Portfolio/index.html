About Me

I am a digital curator with a multidisciplinary background in cognitive science, communication studies, and information studies. Currently pursuing a Master’s in Information Studies at McGill University, I specialize in archives and UX.

I work as a Collections Assistant at the Montreal Museum of Contemporary Art, with previous experience at Festival Accès Asie, the Port Hardy Museum and Archives, and other cultural institutions.
