About Me!

I am a digital curator with a multidisciplinary background in cognitive science and communication studies who is currently pursuing a Master’s in Information Studies at McGill University. I specialize in archives and UX.

I work as a Collections Assistant at the Montreal Museum of Contemporary Art, with previous experience at Festival Accès Asie, the Port Hardy Museum and Archives, and other cultural institutions.

I am passionate about creating accessible and inclusive cultural heritage experiences. Skilled in documentation, cataloguing, research, and digital tools, I aim to bridge technology and culture to reimagine how communities engage with special collections.
